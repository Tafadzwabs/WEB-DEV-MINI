About Me

Benedict Tafadzwanashe Samhika's story is one of dynamic pursuits and a deep-rooted passion for both technology and aviation. Growing up in Nairobi, Kenya, Benedict's journey began with a solid educational foundation at Strathmore University, where he pursued a Diploma in Business Information Technology. His coursework not only encompassed the technical aspects of data structures, algorithms, and software engineering but also delved into the realms of marketing, entrepreneurship, and finance, reflecting his diverse interests and holistic approach to learning. Driven by a thirst for knowledge and adventure, Benedict embarked on a parallel path, earning his Private and Commercial Pilot's Licences from 99s Flying School in Nairobi. This unique combination of technical expertise in IT and aviation underscores Benedict's multidisciplinary mindset and his willingness to explore different domains.
